<p>The Federal Electoral Boundaries Commission for Ontario is holding public hearings, in person and virtually, to gather comments and feedback on the<span> </span><a href="https://redecoupage-redistribution-2022.ca/com/on/prop/index_e.aspx">proposed boundaries and electoral district names</a>.</p>
<h2>How to participate</h2>
<p>If you wish to make a representation at a hearing, you must complete a<span> </span><a href="https://redecoupage-redistribution-2022.ca/com/on/phrg/form/index_e.aspx">Public Hearing Participation Form</a>. The Commission has set<span> </span><strong>September 25, 2022</strong><span> </span>as the date by which the Public Participation Hearing Form is to be filed.</p>
<p>A submission may be made in writing as an alternative to appearing at an in-person or virtual hearing.</p>
<p>Should you wish to send comments and feedback to the Commission, without attending a public hearing, you can do so by mail or email, or by using the<span> </span><a href="https://redecoupage-redistribution-2022.ca/com/on/rim/index_e.aspx">Interactive Mapping Tool</a>.</p>
<p><a class="phearing" href="https://redecoupage-redistribution-2022.ca/com/on/phrg/form/index_e.aspx">Public Hearing Participation Form</a></p>
<p>The Public Hearing Participation Form, or alternatively written submissions (for those who only wish to make a submission in writing), may be filed by email or by mail to:</p>
