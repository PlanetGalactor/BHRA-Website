<h2>New School</h2>
<p><strong> 30 Mulham Pl, Etobicoke, ON M9A 4W1</strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p><h3><span style="color: #9b287b;">Overview</span></h3>
<p><span>Buttonwood Hill School was purchased by the Toronto Catholic District School Board on March 8, 2019. The site was ranked as #1 on the 2019-2020 Capital Priorities list, as a school was desperately needed in central Etobicoke. On October 2, 2019, our Board met with Trustee Markus de Domenico and confirmed our request for a community consultation meeting to be held in our neighbourhood.</span></p>
