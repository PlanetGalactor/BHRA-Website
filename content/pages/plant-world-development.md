<p>&nbsp;</p><h3><span style="color: #9b287b;">Overview</span></h3>
<p><span>Plant World closed its doors as of September 30, 2019. Now known as Notting Hill Condominiums by Lanterra Developments, the site features a master-planned community. Inspired by the famous London neighbourhood, Notting Hill Condominiums will offer an English-inspired lifestyle featuring lush gardens, retail, and amenity spaces. The development comprises multiple residential towers encompassing significant new housing and modern amenities.</span></p>
<p><span>Additionally, a brand new City of Toronto park is being constructed at the site (4000 Eglinton Avenue West). This new public park will serve both the new residents and the broader existing neighbourhood, expanding green space in our community.</span></p>
