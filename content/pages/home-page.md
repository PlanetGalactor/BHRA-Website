<p style="text-align: center;">Buttonwood Hill Residents Association was formed in 2003 when our neighbourhood became aware of a proposed development at 75 Lemonwood Drive. It was clear that our neighbours were concerned about the proposed height, density and increased traffic in the area. Since then, we have advocated for various issues in our neighbourhood, including traffic concerns and proposed new developments. Our mission is to advocate for you, our community, our neighbours and to ensure that you are given an opportunity to have your voices heard.</p>
