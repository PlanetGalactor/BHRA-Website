# How to Post to the BHRA Website

This guide is for Buttonwood Hill Residents Association committee members who want to publish new news items or updates to the website.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Log In**: Go to [`buttonwoodhillresidents.com/admin`](https://buttonwoodhillresidents.com/admin) in your web browser.
2. **Authenticate**: Click the button to log in with your **GitHub account**. 
   *(Note: If you don't have one, create a free GitHub account first and ensure you are given access to the repository).*
3. **Dashboard**: You will see a dashboard with "Posts" and "Pages" on the left sidebar. Click on **Posts**.
4. **New Post**: Click the **New Post** button in the top right.
5. **Fill in the Details**: 
   - Enter your **Title**
   - Pick the **Publish Date**
   - Select the **Category** from the dropdown (e.g., Eglinton West LRT, Humbertown Plaza, etc.)
   - Write your **Excerpt** (a short summary shown on the news grid)
   - Write the main **Body** of the post using the visual text editor (or Markdown).
   - *Optional:* Upload a **Featured Image** or **Attached PDF**.
6. **Publish**: Click the **Publish** button at the top of the page.
7. **Wait for Deployment**: The site will automatically build and deploy your changes. Wait about **1-2 minutes**, then refresh the live website to see your new post!

## Tips
- Keep file sizes for images as small as possible to ensure the site stays fast.
- If you attach a PDF, it will appear as a download link at the bottom of the post.
